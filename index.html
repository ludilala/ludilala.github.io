About us

Learning, sharing and growing. Ludilala World is looking forwards to collaborate with many of you!

We are an innovative enterprise committed to the research and services of children's educational products. Our group has dedicated itself to children's educational products for more than a decade, yielding a wide variety of knowledge for children that incorporates teaching into playing.All our products are available all around the world.

Ludilala World’s group is comprised of a bunch of great illustrators, computer programmers, excellent designers of creative product ideas, and education experts.

Individual Collaboration

 
No matter who you are, parents, college students or children with rich imaginations, you can join Ludilala World as long as you have basic finger painting knowledge and love expressing things by wielding brushes, such as any imaginative image, picture book, story, animal or figure, and as long as you're willing to share and bring happiness to others. Let us spread happiness to all the children around the world!

Copyright Collaboration

 
Ludilala World hopes to work together with all its friends to help children learn happily, share happily and grow happily. In addition, the creators of noted cartoon brands, copyright owners and publishers of picture books, owners of brand images, and children's educational institutions are all warmly welcome to develop intensive collaboration with Ludilala World, enabling them to share what they have with children around the world through this platform.

Business Collaboration

 
We are eagerly looking forward to developing intensive collaboration with worldwide creators, publishers of picture books, renowned children's educational institutions, toy production enterprises and so on. Through the Ludilala World platform, more famous brands can share what they have with children around the world.

Learning, sharing and growing. Ludilala World is looking forwards to collaborate with many of you!
